Platforms: PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, PC
Release Date: February 25, 2022

From the minds of Hidetaka Miyazaki and George R.R. Martin comes Elden Ring, a game that stands as a testament to what open-world gaming can achieve. With its intricate world-building, challenging combat, and a story steeped in dark fantasy, Elden Ring offers an experience that is both deeply immersive and profoundly challenging.

Pros

  • Expansive, beautifully crafted open world.
  • Innovative gameplay mechanics that reward exploration and creativity.
  • Deep lore and engaging story elements.

Cons

  • High difficulty may not appeal to all players.
  • Technical issues at launch affecting performance on some platforms.
